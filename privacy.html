Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

PlayTeam ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use PlayTeam:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and profile photo when you register.
  • Phone number: if you choose to verify your account via phone.
  • Location: your city and approximate location to show nearby sports matches. Location is only accessed when you use the app.
  • Sports preferences: the sports you select during onboarding to personalise your experience.
  • Match activity: matches you create, join, or interact with.
  • Device information: device type and push notification token to send you match alerts.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To show you sports matches near your location.
  • To send push notifications about matches you've joined or been invited to.
  • To allow other players to find and join your matches.
  • To improve the app experience over time.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase – database, authentication, and file storage. Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In – optional login method. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In – optional login method. Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps – to display match locations on a map. Privacy Policy
  • Expo / EAS – push notification delivery. Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Access to your data is restricted to what is necessary to provide the service.

5. Photos & Media

If you upload a profile photo, it is stored securely in our Supabase storage bucket. We only access your photo library when you explicitly choose to upload a photo. We do not access your camera roll without your permission.

6. Location Data

We request access to your device location solely to show sports matches near you. We do not track your location in the background. You can revoke location access at any time in your device settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

PlayTeam is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
